Lost in Nagoya

Posted on 2019-11-25 | | Visitors: |
Words count in article: 716 | Reading time ≈ 3

Intro

Shanghai Office organized an outing by the end of August. I was excited when I heard the news. Since I graduated from the Uni, I was supposed to have a graduation trip.But unfortunately, I was just going straight to work.I was like, oh, finally I got a short break from busy work even if I haven’t done my internal project.

Before we leave for Nagoya, I watched a video called Top 5 things to do in Nagoya on Youtube.

Youtube Link

Nagoya has a rich cultural history and convenient metro system. But I am the one who has zero interest in experiencing the cultural part.So basically we spent the whole 4 days for eating, drinking and shopping.

Try some of the Top1 foods in Nagoya.Those are the food that should not be overlooked.

一蘭:

Crazy, I eat this shit for late night snacks every night. It was Super delicious. Of course, I just put on my slippers and go downstairs then I can eat it. Convenience is a priority.

矢场 pork chop rice:

As the No.1 pork chops in Nagoya, It didn’t shock me. Perhaps I’m not into miso’s flavour.

Barbecue:

I have to say the quality of Japanese beef lives up to its reputation. We went a local restaurant and had a great time. Japanese plum wine and beer are pretty good. On my last night in Japan, I went to a local Izakaya. I wasted a lot of time communicating with the shop assistants in English. Japanese beer is fabulous.

写在后面

从前对日本生鲜和寡淡的调味不太感冒。这次的团建之旅，日本的食物，从便利店到餐馆，美食的品质和口味都很出色，很少有踩雷的时候。大概也是国人愿意扎堆和多次前往旅行的原因吧。

“在天黑以后，往热闹地方躲，跟着别人努力快活，
可惜心里头，有定时的闹钟，提醒你有多寂寞。” ——摘自一篇豆瓣影评
对日本抱有好奇也是源于一部电影《Lost In Translation》,译名是迷失东京。过气电视明星鲍勃，和Scarlett演的夏洛特，在机缘巧合下在东京相遇。在这个不是故乡的陌生城市里，他们在旅馆邂逅，不约而同的失眠，互相陪伴，生活一方面仿佛变的缤纷起来，另一面却逐渐走向迷失。

虽然这次去的是名古屋而不是东京，但作为日本第三大城市，名古屋市中心的工作日和上海一样压抑，地铁飞驰，挤不进去的班车，人行道上健步如飞的上班族。不同的国家，不同的城市却有着相同的压抑感。行程的后半段我们去了当地的一个非遗的村落叫白川乡，即便没有发达的设施，日本的乡下和国内还是有着很不一样的风景和人文。旅行的意义除了放空自己，感受一下不同环境下的人是如何生活也是一番乐趣。暂时结束了学生的生活，希望至少每年都有一到二次的出行机会，去探索一下不同的人文，风景和美食吧。

日本，还会再来的。
